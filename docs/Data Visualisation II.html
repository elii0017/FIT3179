Australia's Corrective Services

This dashboard explores data on Australia's corrective services, visualising trends in imprisonment rates, prisoner demographics

Australians in Custody

In the recent June quarter, 44,051 individuals were in custody. An increase of 2% of 746 more individuals since the March quarter.

Whilst the graph appears to "fluctuate" due to the y-axis starting at 40,000, this was done to better visualise the increase and decrease of custody numbers.

The lowest amount of Australians in custody between the periods of 2019 to 2024 was 40,330 in the March Quarter of 2022

Australia's Daily Imprisonment Rate

Australia's Jun Qtr 2024 Imprisonment Rate was 208 persons per 100,000 in the adult population. The Northen Territory has the highest imprisonment rate (<1000) out of any other state or territory. The map does not display the change in March Qtr 2024 or June Qtr 2024 as it does not adequately display the change in daily imprisonment rates across each state.
